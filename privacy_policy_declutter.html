1. Introduction

Declutter App ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you use our mobile application, Declutter App (the "App"), available on iOS, Android, and web platforms.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using the Declutter App, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Item Data:

  • Photos of personal items you add to the app
  • Item titles, descriptions, categories, quantities, and estimated values
  • Notes and metadata associated with each item
  • Pile assignments (Keep, Donate, Sell, Discard, Recycle)

Usage Data:

  • Item management actions (add, edit, move, complete, delete)
  • Decision history related to decluttering choices
  • Calendar events and scheduling information you create

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device Information:

  • Device type, model, and operating system
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App version and build number

Usage Analytics:

  • App interactions and feature usage
  • Session duration and frequency
  • Crash reports and performance metrics
  • Feature engagement data

Location Information:

  • Approximate location (if enabled for donation center search features)
  • This is only collected with your explicit permission

2.3 Photos and Media

  • Photos you capture or upload are stored locally on your device
  • Photos may be retained in the app's local database for archival and historical purposes
  • We do not automatically upload photos to external servers unless you specifically enable cloud backup features

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality - Storing and organizing your items, managing piles, and tracking decluttering progress
  • Reminders & Notifications - Sending you reminders about items pending action and motivational notifications based on your preferences
  • Analytics & Improvement - Understanding how you use the app to improve features and user experience
  • Performance & Reliability - Detecting, diagnosing, and addressing technical issues
  • Notifications - Sending push notifications for reminders, streaks, achievements, and app updates (only with your consent)
  • Location Services - Finding nearby donation centers or drop-off locations (only with your explicit permission)
  • Backup & Sync - Synchronizing your data across devices if you enable cloud backup

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Local Storage

  • Primary Storage: The majority of your data (items, photos, metadata) is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage
  • Offline Access: Your data is accessible offline; synchronization occurs when connectivity is available

4.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

  • If you enable cloud backup through cloud sync features (future feature), your data may be encrypted and stored on secure cloud servers
  • You control whether cloud backup is enabled
  • Cloud backups are encrypted in transit and at rest

4.3 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure data storage on your device
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only

Note: No security system is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

5.1 On Your Device

  • Your item data, photos, and metadata remain stored on your device until you choose to delete them
  • You have full control over deletion of individual items and can perform factory reset to delete all app data
  • Archived/completed items are retained for your reference unless you manually delete them

5.2 Cloud Backups

  • If cloud backup is enabled, backups are retained according to your backup settings
  • You can delete cloud backups at any time through the app's settings
  • Backups expire after 365 days of inactivity (configurable)

5.3 Analytics & Logs

  • Anonymous usage analytics may be retained for up to 90 days
  • Crash reports are retained for up to 30 days
  • Personal identifiable information is removed from analytics after 30 days

6. Information Sharing & Disclosure

6.1 What We Do NOT Do

We do NOT sell, trade, rent, or otherwise disclose your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

6.2 Sharing With Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the app, provided they:

  • Have agreed to maintain the confidentiality of your information
  • Are restricted from using your information for purposes other than providing services to us
  • Include services such as:
    • Cloud storage providers (if cloud backup is enabled)
    • Analytics providers (with anonymized data only)
    • Crash reporting services
    • Payment processors (for future monetization features)

6.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes
  • Enforce our Terms of Service or other agreements
  • Protect against fraud, security, or technical issues
  • Protect the safety, rights, or property of our users, the public, or the Company

6.4 Integration with External Services

7. Your Privacy Rights & Choices

7.1 Access to Your Information

  • You have the right to access, review, and download all your personal information stored in the app at any time
  • You can export your data in a machine-readable format (CSV/PDF)

7.2 Correction & Modification

  • You can modify or correct any item data directly within the app
  • Changes are reflected immediately in your local storage

7.3 Deletion of Your Information

  • You can delete individual items at any time
  • You can delete all app data through your device's app settings (uninstall/clear data)
  • You can request deletion of cloud backups through the app's settings
  • Upon deletion, data is permanently removed from your device and our servers

7.4 Opt-Out Options

  • Notifications: You can disable push notifications in your device settings or the app's notification settings
  • Location Services: You can revoke location permissions in your device settings
  • Analytics: You can opt out of analytics collection in the app's privacy settings
  • Cloud Backup: You can disable cloud backup at any time

7.5 Do Not Track Signals

Your device or browser may send "Do Not Track" signals. We honor these signals and will not engage in tracking that allows targeted advertising based on your online activities.

8. Children's Privacy

If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

Parents or guardians who believe a child has provided information to us should contact us immediately.

12. California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following additional rights:

  • Right to Know: You can request what personal information is collected, used, and shared
  • Right to Delete: You can request deletion of personal information collected from you
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information (note: we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to Correct: You can request correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights

California users can submit privacy requests through our app's settings or by contacting us (see Section 14).

13. European Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, we process your data under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: Access all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we process your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Export your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection authority

Legal Basis for Processing:

  • Performance of a service you request (storage and organization of your items)
  • Your explicit consent (for optional features like cloud backup)
  • Our legitimate interests (improving and maintaining the app)
  • Compliance with legal obligations
